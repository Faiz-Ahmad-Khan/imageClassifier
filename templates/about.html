Architecture / Principle of the project

Image classifiers use Convolutional Neural Networks because of its high accuracy. The CNN follows a hierarchical model which works on building a network, like a funnel, and finally gives out a fully-connected layer where all the neurons are connected to each other and the output is processed


The Layers involved in Convolutional Neural Network

  2. Pooling
  3. Flattening
  4. Full Connection
